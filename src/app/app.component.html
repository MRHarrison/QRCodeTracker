About

This proof of concept will be demonstrating decentralization, open access and trust technologies for the tracking of an apple. This site is hosted via IPFS which decentralizes hosting for our SPA (single page application) across many different nodes, like bittorrent for hosting. We are also using ethereum blockchain technologies for contract transactions and as our data store.
  • Decentralization Anyone can scan the apple and get info of where it has been.
  • Open Access/Transparnecy Anyone can scan the apple and get info of where it has been. Anyone will be able to access all of the apple's, no need to create api's for your data. Consumers, you, your company and whoever has access
  • Trust Using a blockchain contract ensures the data is validated by a third-party. Having open access data also ensures trust with you and your consumers

Limitations
  1. Transactions on the ethereum blockchain are expensive, but as the technology progresses the prices will come down.
  2. Mobile Camera - The example flow above to use the computers camera and not the phone camera is awkward, because there is no blockchain browser with camera access. The other option was to build an ios/android scanner, but that was going to far outside of this demo.
  3. HTTPSIn order to use the camera and location, our IPFS app must have a secure origin.
