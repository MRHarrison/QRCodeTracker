About

This proof of concept will be using blockchain and IPFS, better known as a Dapp, to demonstrate the benefits and limitations of decentralization and blockchain contracts when tracking items. This web application is hosted via IPFS and the contracts and data store is leveraging Solidity and the Ethereum blockchain. In short IPFS is like bittorrent for websites and Ethereum is an immutable database with contract execution. We are using ethereum because of it's solidity interface, strong community and strong future.

Strengths

  • Open Access/Transparency Consumers, you, your company and whoever wants access can get access the tracking of your products. Anyone can scan the apple and get info of where it has been. This kind of transparency will be trust to your brand.
  • Decentralized Infrastructure Information will be duplicated over many nodes, delivering both resiliency and data validation. You no longer need to support infrastructure, you only pay for what you use.

Limitations

  • Transactions on the blockchain are still expensive and slow, but as the technology progresses the prices will come down. Ethereum is also planning on having sharding available later this year to support thousands of transactions per second.
  • Wallet/Blockchain Must have a wallet connected to scan and save your location and UI. Most people have no idea of how to connect to the blockchain, so apps need to be made and hopefully big players like Google and Apple will connect users through Chrome and Safari
  • Mobile Camera - The example flow above to use the computer's camera and not the phone camera is awkward, I implemented it this way because there is no blockchain browser with camera access. Just another indicator of how new this ecosystem is.
