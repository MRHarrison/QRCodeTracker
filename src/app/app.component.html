Proof Of Concept Demo

Steps
  1. You need to have the metamask chrome extension installed, and on ROPSTEN Test Net. Get free test eth here.
  2. Use Your Phone's Camera To Scan QR Code to the right
  3. Imagine your phone is the item that you are tracking.
  4. Now click this and scan the QR Code on your phone.
  5. Allow permission to get location, so it can be recorded to the blockchain.
  6. Location will be saved to blockchain

Limitations
  1. Transactions on the ethereum blockchain are expensive, but as the technology progresses the prices will come down.
  2. The example flow above to use the computers camera and not the phone camera is awkward, because there is no blockchain browser with camera access. The other option was to build an ios/android scanner, but that was going to far outside of this demo.
